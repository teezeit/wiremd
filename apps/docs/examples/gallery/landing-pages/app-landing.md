- `sketch` - Quick prototype for app concept
- `clean` - Modern app marketing page
- `wireframe` - Structure for development team
- `tailwind` - Vibrant, contemporary mobile feel
- `material` - Google Play aesthetic
- `brutal` - Bold, distinctive app branding
- `none` - Semantic HTML for React/Vue

**Generate this example:**
```bash
wiremd app-landing.md --style sketch
wiremd app-landing.md --style material -o app-material.html
wiremd app-landing.md --style tailwind -o app-tailwind.html
```
